Quality and Reliability comparison

Both Toyota and Honda cars are generally considered to have excellent quality.
However, Toyota scores slightly higher in many of its car offerings when it comes to reliability.
Honda is a smaller company than Toyota, but it is the biggest manufacturer of engines. So when you look at the engine reliability, Honda has a edge against Toyota. You can check this in the Table below.
